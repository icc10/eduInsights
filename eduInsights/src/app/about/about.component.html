The Product

The purpose of EDU Insights is to give instructors a chance to learn from their practices and improve. Students are given the chance to express their opinions on course matter such as the speed at which content is being covered or the difficulty of assignments. By the click of a button, an instructor is able to generate a class code which students can enter to submit their responses. Instructors can evaluate these responses themselves to develop the next best course of action or they can take advantage of our generated sugguestions for how they can improve. We can feed responses to AI to form their own tips to improve the class, helping both students and professors to make the most of their semester!
